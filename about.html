         About Me

                       Mahesh Gokhale


My name is Mahesh Gokhale and I am a student of MITWPU, Kothrud, Pune. I belong to the branch of Electronics and Communication engineering, which is a sub-discipline of electrical engineering and is distinguished by the additional use of active components such as semiconductor devices to amplify and control electric current flow. My interests are in core electronics, specifically robotics and automation as well as in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
My hobbies are gaming, listening to music, solving puzzles, reading books, sketching and so on. I also like to travel once in a while...
I have experience in working with various microcontrollers such as C8051, RaspberryPi, Arduino, etc and I have also built projects and worked with elctronic active components along with these controllers as well as individually. I plan to work in a company which will best fit my abilities. I will be providing links to my future projects below. Wish me all the best for future endevours... :)
